DropWebP

DropWebP is a drag-and-drop tool for converting image files to WebP format.

What is WebP?

WebP is a next-generation image format developed by Google and is compatible with all current browsers as of 2021. Google, the developer of WebP, has announced that it can be about 26% lighter than PNG and 25-34% lighter than JPG with almost the same image quality.

When I tried it, I converted an uncompressed 4K quality PNG file that was originally 12.9M to non-degraded WebP, and I was able to reduce it to 7.85MB. The recommended value is 95% lossy compression, but in that case it will be around 1.32M.

Original and Lossless

Lossless compression of an image halves its capacity.

Original and Lossy WebP (95%)

In the case of lossy compression, even if the quality is 95%, the capacity is about 1/10.

Lossy WebP and JPEG highest

The capacity of WebP at the time of lossless compression is almost the same as the capacity at the time of the highest quality of JPEG.
